📌 How to Get Gmail App Password:
  1. Go to Google App Passwords
  2. Sign in to your Google Account
  3. Select "Mail" as the app and "Windows Computer" as the device
  4. Click "Generate" and copy the 16-character password
  5. Paste it in the "Email Password" field below
⚠️ Important: Never use your regular Gmail password. Always use an App Password for security.

ℹ️ About Notifications

PharmaAlert will automatically check for expiring medicines and low stock every day at 9:00 AM and send email notifications if any issues are found.

What you'll be notified about:

  • Medicines that have already expired
  • Medicines expiring within 30 days (Critical)
  • Medicines expiring within 31-90 days (Warning)
  • Medicines expiring within 91+ days (Notice)
  • Low stock medicines (below threshold)
