Report Information

  • All Medicines Inventory: Shows all SKUs with their batch count and total stock.
  • Near-Expiry Medicines: Medicines expiring within the next 6 months (configured via EXPIRY_LEAD_DAYS in .env).
  • Already Expired: Medicines that have exceeded their expiry date.
  • Low Stock Alert: Medicines with stock below their configured low-stock threshold.
  • Usage Log: Transaction history of all stock movements (receipts, issues) for the past 30 days.

Download Format

All reports are exported as CSV (Comma-Separated Values) files that can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application.
